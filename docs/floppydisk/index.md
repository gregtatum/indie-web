# Floppy Disk Docs

Floppy Disk is an indie web project for creative documents that live on your
storage, not inside someone elseâ€™s platform. Keep notes, drafts, maps, and
research in plain files that you can move, back up, or remix anytime.

## Indie web principles

- Own the data. Your files are stored in a real folder on your storage.
- Use open formats. Markdown, PDFs, images, and text all remain portable.
- Avoid lock-in. You can move or sync your files without exporting.
- Keep it personal. Your workspace is yours, whether it is local, Dropbox, or a
  NAS.

## What you can do

- Write and read Markdown with a live preview.
- Drop in images, PDFs, and other files for reference.
- Organize folders for projects, campaigns, or research.
- Keep everything offline-first, with optional sync.
